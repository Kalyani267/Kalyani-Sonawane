Overview

Course Objectives :

    1. Understand the fundamentals of online journalism, including storytelling techniques, digital newsgathering, and multimedia reporting.
    2. Build a professional online journalism portfolio showcasing your work and accomplishments throughout the course.
    3. Gain practical experience through hands-on assignments, projects, and case studies that simulate real-world journalism scenarios and challenges.
    4. Receive personalized feedback, guidance, and mentorship from experienced journalists and media professionals.

Course Structure :

    1. Introduction to Online Journalism
    2. Social Media Journalism and Audience Engagement
    3. Ethics and Standards in Online Journalism
    4. Building Your Online Journalism Portfolio
Course Duration : The course is self-paced and typically spans 2 weeks allowing participants to complete the coursework at their own convenience while balancing other commitments.
